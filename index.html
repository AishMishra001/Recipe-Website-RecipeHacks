1.Gulab Jamun

Ingredients:

  • Khoya (reduced milk)
  • Paneer (cottage cheese)
  • All-purpose flour
  • Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Baking soda
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cardamom powder
  • Saffron strands

Recipe

  1. Mix khoya, paneer, and a pinch of baking soda to form a smooth dough.
  2. Shape the dough into small balls.
  3. Fry the balls until golden brown.
  4. Make sugar syrup by boiling sugar, water, cardamom powder, and saffron.
  5. Soak the fried balls in the sugar syrup.
  6. Allow them to absorb the syrup, and Gulab Jamuns are ready to be served.

2. Jalebi

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Yogurt
  • Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Saffron strands
  • Baking soda
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cardamom powder

Recipe

  1. Mix flour, yogurt, and a pinch of baking soda to make a thick batter.
  2. Allow the batter to ferment for a few hours.
  3. Make a sugar syrup by boiling sugar, water, cardamom powder, and saffron.
  4. Heat ghee in a pan and pour the batter into a spiral shape.
  5. Fry until crispy and then dip the fried jalebis into the sugar syrup.
  6. Let them soak for a while, and delicious Jalebis are ready.

3. Rasgulla

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Lemon juice or vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cardamom powder

Recipe

  1. Boil milk and curdle it using lemon juice or vinegar.
  2. Strain the chenna (curdled milk) and knead it into a smooth dough.
  3. Shape the dough into small balls.
  4. Boil sugar and water to make a syrup.
  5. Drop the chenna balls into the syrup and cook until they double in size.
  6. Sprinkle cardamom powder, and Rasgullas are ready to be served.

4.Barfi

Ingredients:

  • Khoya (reduced milk)
  • Sugar
  • Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Cardamom powder
  • Almonds or pistachios (for garnish)

Recipe

  1. Cook khoya in ghee until it thickens.
  2. Add sugar and cardamom powder, continue cooking until
    the mixture leaves the sides of the pan.
  3. Spread the mixture on a greased plate and let it cool.
  4. Cut into diamond-shaped pieces.
  5. Garnish with chopped almonds or pistachios.

5.Ladoo

Ingredients:

  • Besan (gram flour)
  • Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, cashews)

Recipe

  1. Roast besan in ghee until it turns golden brown.
  2. Add sugar and cardamom powder to the roasted besan.
  3. Mix well and let it cool slightly.
  4. Shape the mixture into round ladoos.
  5. Garnish with chopped nuts, and the delicious Besan Ladoos are ready.
